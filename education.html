Education



Lluis leads ensembles and workshops on a weekly basis across the country in a variety of contexts, and also leads many education projects. He is a private music teacher (saxophone, clarinet and flute), teaching pupils of all ages and abilities, and also teaches improvisation, music theory and coaches jazz ensembles. You can contact Lluis with any enquiries about receiving tuition on saxophones or clarinets (from complete beginner through to degree level, both standard instrumental lessons or improvisation focused), or for any enquiries on workshops or ensemble coaching, on the contact page of this website, or by clicking onto his tutor page on the musicteachers.co.uk website.












Some of Lluis' recent and current educational work includes;


- Visiting Tutor for the Birmingham Conservatoire Junior Jazz Department

- Musical Director of University of Birmingham Jazz Orchestra (2013-2015)

- Artistic Director for THSH education project ‘Jazzing About’ with disadvantaged children from inner city schools and nurseries in Birmingham

- improvisation workshops for various education charities, including GBRT

- regular frontman at Symphony Hall’s Musical Picnic series (see above photo)

- coaching sessions with the Guthlaxton College Big Band, Orchestra and other Ensembles

- several drop-in sessions in nurseries and play centres across Birmingham introducing young children to music and movement
